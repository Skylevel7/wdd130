Beginner Rapids

Duration: 2–3 Hours Difficulty Level: Easy Perfect For: Families, kids, first-time rafters Description: Take a relaxing float down the beautiful Clearwater River. This trip is perfect for those who want to experience the joy of rafting without intense rapids. Drift past towering pines, wildlife in their natural habitat, and enjoy calm, crystal-clear waters with just the right splash of excitement. Our experienced guides ensure a fun and safe experience for all ages. Includes: • Professional guide • All safety gear (life vest, helmet, paddle) • Light snacks and bottled water • Free digital photos of your trip

Intermediate Adventure

Duration: Half-Day (4–5 Hours) Difficulty Level: Moderate Perfect For: Active beginners and adventurous groups Description: The Canyon Run is our most popular trip, offering a mix of Class II and III rapids through a scenic canyon filled with towering cliffs, waterfalls, and plenty of excitement. Experience heart-pounding drops and fast turns as you navigate the bends of the River Rush Trail. Ideal for those ready to kick things up a notch! Includes: • Certified rafting guide • All rafting gear • Mid-trip lunch and refreshments • Shuttle to and from the river

Extreme Challenge

Duration: Full Day (6–8 Hours) Difficulty Level: Advanced Perfect For: Experienced rafters and thrill-seekers Description: Brace yourself for an adrenaline-pumping ride down Class IV and V rapids on the infamous Thunder River. This all-day adventure is not for the faint of heart! Expect massive drops, powerful currents, and fast maneuvering under the direction of elite guides. Between the thrills, enjoy breathtaking views and a riverside lunch.
